how it works

Learn More about how our website works

Real-Time CCTV Footage

Our website integrates live CCTV feeds from various key points across Bogor's streets. This allows users to view real-time road conditions and monitor traffic flow. You can access multiple camera views, ensuring a clear understanding of the traffic situation before heading out.

Traffic Density Status

Each road segment is analyzed and color-coded based on the current traffic density, giving you a quick glance at whether the streets are clear, moderately busy, or congested. This data is updated in real-time to help you make informed decisions about your travel route.

Traffic Density Graphs

Visualize traffic trends over time with our interactive traffic density graphs. These graphs display historical data and current traffic fluctuations, allowing users to predict peak hours and plan their trips efficiently.
